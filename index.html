Star Fish

Star Fish have no brain or blood!But they are certainly not fools!! Sea water is pumped throughout thier body as a replacement for blood.

Jelly Fish

Jelly Fish are peculiar!While they don't possess brains,they still have neurons that send all sorts of signals throughout their body.

Sea Urchin

Although Sea Urchins don't have brains it could be their entire nervous system more or less acts as a brain.It is hard to examine their system.
